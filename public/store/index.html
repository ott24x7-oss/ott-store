How fast is the delivery? ▾
Delivery is instant! As soon as your payment is confirmed, you'll receive login credentials via WhatsApp and Email. Most orders are delivered in under 60 seconds.
Are these subscriptions official? ▾
Yes, all subscriptions are fully working and legitimate. These are account sharing plans (family/group plans) which are a common and legal practice. You get a personal profile or individual account that works perfectly on all devices.
What payment methods are accepted? ▾
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, all UPI apps), net banking, and bank transfer. All transactions are secure with instant wallet credit.
What if my subscription stops working? ▾
We offer replacement guarantee! If your subscription stops working during the validity period, contact us on WhatsApp and we'll replace it within hours at no extra cost.
How do I track my order? ▾
Login to your account at My Account → My Orders to see all your orders and credentials. You can also check WhatsApp for instant delivery.
Is there a refund policy? ▾
Yes! We have a 24-hour refund policy for any technical issues. If a subscription doesn't work and we can't replace it, you get a full refund to your wallet. See our Refund Policy.
